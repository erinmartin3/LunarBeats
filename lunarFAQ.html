Frequently Asked Questions

These FAQs cover important information about the festival, from ticketing to event policies, so everyone can have a safe and enjoyable experience.

1. When and where is Lunar Beats Music Festival taking place?

Lunar Beats Music Festival is scheduled for July 21st and 22nd, and it will be held at Pimlico Race Course in Baltimore, Maryland

2. How can I purchase tickets for the festival?

Tickets can be purchased online through our official website or at the festival's entrance if available. We offer a variety of ticket options, including early bird and VIP packages, to suit your preferences.

3. What are the age restrictions for attending the festival?

unar Beats Music Festival is an all-ages event but is targeted towards adults and teenagers. However, attendees under the age of 16 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian.

4. Can I bring my own food and drinks to the festival?

Outside food and beverages are not allowed within the festival grounds. We offer a wide range of food and drink options, including vegetarian and vegan choices, as well as alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.

5. Is there parking available at the venue?

Yes, there will be parking available at the festival venue. However, we recommend carpooling or using public transportation to minimize environmental impact and traffic congestion.

6. Are there any accommodations or camping options available for festival-goers?

There are many nearby hotels for festival attendees.

7. Can I bring my pet to the festival?

Unfortunately, pets are not allowed within the festival grounds, with the exception of registered service animals.

8. What should I do in case of an emergency or if I need medical assistance?

We will have trained medical personnel on-site to provide assistance in case of emergencies. Look for the clearly marked medical tents for help.

9. Can I get a refund if I can't attend the festival after purchasing a ticket?

Tickets are non-refundable. However, you may be able to transfer your ticket to someone else by following our ticket transfer process. Please refer to our ticketing policies for more information.

10. Are there restrictions on what I can bring to the festival, such as bags and backpacks?

Small bags and backpacks are allowed, but they are subject to security checks at the entrance. For everyone's safety, certain items such as weapons and illegal substances are strictly prohibited. Check our website for a list of restricted items.
